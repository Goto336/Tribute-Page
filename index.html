Ada Lovelace

Considered the First Computer Programmer!



Why is/was Ada so important? Because she is considered the first computer programmer because of her knowledge of advance math calculations
Who helped her along the way? Charles Babbage, a mathematician and inventor, served as her mentor
Who is Charles Babbage? Known as the father of the computer, he invented the difference engine, which was meant to perform mathematical calculations
How is this known? In her notes, Lovelace described how codes could be created for the device to handle letters and symbols along with numbers
